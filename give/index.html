Security & Trust

How Your Information
is Protected

PCI Level 1 Certified Payment Processing

We use Stripe — the same payment infrastructure trusted by Amazon, Apple, Google, and millions of businesses worldwide. Stripe is PCI Level 1 certified, the highest level of payment security certification that exists.

Your Card Number is Never Stored

When you enter your card details, that information goes directly from your browser to Stripe's encrypted servers. The Jesus Barn never sees, handles, or stores your card number at any point.

SSL Encrypted Connection

All data transmitted between your browser and this website is encrypted using SSL/TLS — the same technology used by banks and financial institutions worldwide.

Instant Email Receipt

You will receive an email receipt immediately after your gift is processed. Keep it for your records.

100% Refund Guarantee

If The Jesus Barn does not receive city approval and cannot move forward, every donation will be returned at 100% of what was given. Careful documentation is kept of all contributions.

No online system connected to the internet can be guaranteed 100% hack-proof — that's true of every bank, every payment processor, and every website in the world. What we can tell you is that we've used industry-leading security practices to protect your information, and your card data never touches our servers.

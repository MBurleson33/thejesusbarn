Our Heart

Why We Do What We Do

Now
Vision

We believe we should be one body in Christ — and we have an opportunity to gather our neighbors and use our individual gifts and giftings to serve our amazing Piedmont community.

As followers of Christ, we have the answer the world is seeking. And not only that — we each have unique tools and talents to build a brighter future for all who choose to gather with us.

Can you imagine what we could build together with Jesus as our guide?

A Note Before You Partner

The Jesus Barn is currently pending city approval and cannot begin construction or operations until July or August 2026. We want to be fully transparent with our community about where things stand.

In the unlikely event that city approval is not granted, every donation — monetary and physical — will be returned at 100% of what was given. Careful documentation will be kept of all contributions throughout this process.

That said, we believe wholeheartedly that God has placed this vision on our hearts for a reason. We know our God is bigger than any obstacle, and we trust He will provide a way.

Thank you for standing with us in faith.

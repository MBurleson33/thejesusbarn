Overview

The Jesus Barn LLC ("The Jesus Barn," "we," "us," or "our") operates the website located at thejesusbarn.org. We are a community organization based in Piedmont, Oklahoma.

We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what personal information we collect through our website, how we use it, and what rights you have over it. We collect only what we need, we don't sell it, and we use it only to connect with you as a member of our community.

The short version: We collect your name, email, and contact info when you fill out a form on our site. We use it to follow up with you about your interest in The Jesus Barn. We don't sell it, share it with advertisers, or use it for anything you wouldn't expect.

What We Collect

Get Involved Form

When you fill out our partner signup form, we collect:

  • Your full name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number (optional)
  • How you'd like to partner with us (Sow, Serve, Gather, or Work)
  • Any additional information you choose to share in the message field

Community Wall

When you submit a note to our Community Wall, we collect:

  • Your name or a chosen anonymous identifier
  • Your general location (city, optional)
  • Your submitted message

Community Wall submissions are reviewed before they appear publicly. Approved messages may be displayed on our website. You may request removal of your message at any time by contacting us.

Donations (Donorbox)

If you make a donation through our site, you will be directed to or presented with a form powered by Donorbox, a third-party payment processor. Donorbox collects payment and contact information directly and securely. We receive only the information necessary to acknowledge your gift and send a receipt. We do not store your payment card information. Donorbox's privacy policy is available at donorbox.org/privacy.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as government ID numbers, financial account details, health information, or precise geolocation data.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Follow up with you about your interest in partnering with The Jesus Barn
  • Coordinate volunteer schedules, supply donations, or financial gifts
  • Display your approved Community Wall message on our website
  • Send you occasional updates about The Jesus Barn if you have opted in
  • Respond to questions or requests you send us directly

We will never use your information to send unsolicited marketing, sell products, or contact you for any purpose unrelated to The Jesus Barn community.

How We Store Your Information

Form submissions from our website are stored in a private Google Sheets spreadsheet accessible only to authorized members of The Jesus Barn team. This spreadsheet is hosted on Google's servers and protected by Google Workspace security standards.

We retain your information for as long as it is relevant to your involvement with The Jesus Barn. If you would like your information removed, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

We do not use a traditional database or server to store your personal information. All form data is stored in a private Google Sheet that is not publicly accessible.

Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data:

Google Sheets & Google Apps Script

We use Google Sheets to store form submissions and Google Apps Script to process them. Data is transmitted securely via HTTPS. Google's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. Google Analytics collects anonymized data about page views, time on site, and general geographic location. It does not collect your name or contact information. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on. Google's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.

Donorbox

Donations are processed through Donorbox, which uses Stripe or PayPal for secure payment processing. Donorbox's privacy policy is available at donorbox.org/privacy.

Google Fonts

Our website uses Google Fonts to display typography. When you visit our site, your browser may make a request to Google's servers to load these fonts. Google may collect your IP address as part of this request. Google's privacy policy applies.

Vercel (Website Hosting)

Our website is hosted on Vercel. Vercel may collect basic access logs including IP addresses for security and performance purposes. Vercel's privacy policy is available at vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy.

We do not share your personal information with any third party for marketing, advertising, or commercial purposes.

Cookies & Tracking

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. Here is how our site uses them:

Essential Cookies

Our website itself does not set any cookies. However, third-party services embedded in our site may set cookies as part of their normal operation.

Analytics Cookies (Google Analytics)

Google Analytics sets cookies to track anonymous usage data such as which pages you visit and how long you spend on the site. These cookies do not identify you personally. The primary cookies used are:

  • _ga — distinguishes unique users. Expires after 2 years.
  • _ga_[ID] — maintains session state. Expires after 2 years.
  • _gid — distinguishes users. Expires after 24 hours.

Payment Cookies (Donorbox)

If you interact with a Donorbox donation form, Donorbox may set cookies to maintain your session and prevent fraud. These are governed by Donorbox's cookie policy.

How to Control Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some features on our site. You can also opt out of Google Analytics specifically using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

We do not use advertising cookies, retargeting pixels, or social media tracking pixels at this time. If this changes, we will update this policy.

Your Rights

Regardless of where you are located, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access — You may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction — You may ask us to correct inaccurate information.
  • Deletion — You may ask us to delete your personal information. We will do so promptly unless we are required by law to retain it.
  • Opt-out — You may opt out of any communications from us at any time by contacting us directly.
  • Community Wall removal — You may request that your Community Wall submission be removed at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@thejesusbarn.org. We will respond within 30 days.

California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete it, and the right to opt out of the sale of your personal information. We do not sell personal information. To exercise your CCPA rights, contact us at hello@thejesusbarn.org.

Children's Privacy

The Jesus Barn is a family-friendly community space welcoming people of all ages. However, our website's forms are intended for adults (18 and older) or minors with parental consent. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent.

If you believe a child under 13 has submitted personal information through our website without parental consent, please contact us at hello@thejesusbarn.org and we will remove it promptly.

Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as our website or services change. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of our website after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, please reach out. We're a small community organization and we're happy to talk through any concerns.
